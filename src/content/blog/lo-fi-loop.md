There's a specific frequency where background music stops being music and becomes texture. Lo-fi hip hop lives exactly there. It's not silence and it's not distraction — it's a third thing.

I write better with it on. Code better too. The repetition creates a container for focus without demanding attention. The beats are predictable enough to ignore and varied enough to not feel mechanical.

This site's lo-fi mood tries to capture that. Warm amber gradients, slow animation speeds, gentle glow. The visual equivalent of a beat you've heard a thousand times that still works.
