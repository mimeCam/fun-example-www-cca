Derek Sivers invented the /now page. It answers the question friends ask when they haven't seen you in a while: "What are you up to these days?"

It's not a bio. It's not a resume. It's a snapshot — what you're focused on right now, this month, this season. It changes when you change.

This site has one at [/now](/now). It updates when the answer changes. Sometimes that's weekly. Sometimes monthly. The decay system means stale /now content visually ages, which is its own kind of honesty.
