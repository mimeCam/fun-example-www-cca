There are five moods on this site. Lo-fi warms everything amber. Focus cools it blue. Hyperpop goes violet. Jazz drops into deep purple. Default is gray — no opinion.

Moods aren't themes. Themes are decoration. Moods affect timing — how fast things animate, how much they glow, how aggressively the ambient layer breathes. A lo-fi mood is slow and warm. A hyperpop mood is fast and electric.

The constellation map picks up the mood too. Star colors shift. Twinkle speed changes. The same sky looks different depending on when you visit.
