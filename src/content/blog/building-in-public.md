Most personal sites are finished products. This one isn't. You're watching the construction — the scaffolding is part of the architecture.

Building in public means every rough edge is visible. The half-finished pages, the placeholder text, the features that don't quite work yet. That's intentional. A blog that pretends to be complete on day one is lying about its own history.

The constellation map on this site will grow with every post. Each piece of writing becomes a star, automatically. No manual wiring. The sky fills itself.
